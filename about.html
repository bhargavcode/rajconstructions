About

Raj Construction delivers expert construction solutions specializing in sanitary, plumbing, fire-fighting, water treatment, and civil works for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Built on Expertise, Driven by Quality

Since its inception in 2008, Raj Construction has been delivering excellence in civil and plumbing works. Our expertise spans internal and external water supply, drainage systems, sanitary works, fire-fighting solutions, and water treatment plants.

We specialize in complete project execution with high-quality materials, professional installation, and adherence to safety standards. From core cutting and external infrastructure works to Mono Block & MSMO fire pumps and treatment plants, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to client needs. Our experienced team ensures projects are completed on time, within budget, and with maximum client satisfaction.
