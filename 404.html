About Raj Construction

Raj Construction was incorporated on 01/09/2008 and is promoted by Mr. Raj Kumar Bhargavas. The company is founded on the principles of providing excellent professional service. Rapid growth in the infrastructure sector and consistent GDP growth has prompted Raj Construction to take up new challenges in plumbing works for high-rise apartments and commercial constructions. Our motto is to exceed customer expectations by understanding their needs, delivering specified quality within agreed timelines, and becoming a trusted partner.

At Raj Construction, we believe the workplace should be a space to unleash creative excellence, bounded only by one's conscience. We are proud to have created a promising environment for our team.

Our Vision

To provide value-added construction services, transforming our company into a leading MEPF service sector firm with ethical standards. Committed to quality, reliability, trust, and customer satisfaction, we aim to build lasting relationships with our clients and foster a culture of total quality and continuous improvement among employees.

Quality Policy

Quality at Raj Construction means doing the job right the first time. We are committed to customer satisfaction and loyalty, reducing cost and cycle times through efficient resource use. We strive for complete client satisfaction by delivering quality solutions on time while maintaining impeccable safety standards. Our keywords are: QUALITY, TIME, and TRUST.

Management
